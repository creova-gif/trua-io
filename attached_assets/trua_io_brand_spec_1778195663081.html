Brand voice guidelines
Professional & authoritative
Write with confidence. No hedging, no filler. Every sentence earns its place. Use active voice. Treat the reader as a competent business professional.
Culturally grounded
Reference Tanzanian cities, industries, and business culture naturally. Never condescending. Swahili phrases welcome where appropriate. Acknowledge the EAC market context.
Outcome-first
Lead with results, not features. "Book 3× more meetings" beats "AI email drafting engine." Every user-facing message should answer: what does this do for me?
